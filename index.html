Biography

Sergio Jimenez is a young spanish dj and producer known artistically as Serz. that has been playing for more than 12 years. During this years has grown between all kinds of electronic music, from tech-house to big room. All this time has made him a talented experienced dj and producer, who enjoys on the decks and puts an extra effort on set preparation creating a bunch of own mashups, bootlegs, remixes... this combined with the creativity and easiness to remix tracks on the fly , makes the difference in front other djâ€™s and also between his own sets tending to be different every time. During this time has released tracks, and made tones of un- officials remixes, been part of a radio team, where was a radio announcer running his own program. Nowadays is well-known for being a contundent dj, playing mostly progressive house, big room, and always including crowd-breakers as nirvana, cold- play, u2... always trying to make the people be part of the show, singing, jumping....
